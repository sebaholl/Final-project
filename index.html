TREATY

The Global Plastics Treaty aims to reduce plastic waste by regulating production and boosting recycling systems.

The treaty seeks to protect vulnerable communities disproportionately affected by plastic pollution worldwide.

By supporting international cooperation, the treaty ensures that all countries work together to fight plastic pollution.

The Impact of Plastic Pollution

Plastic pollution harms ecosystems, human health, and the climate.

The Global Plastics Treaty offers solutions
through recycling and sustainable practices.

In 2019, plastics accounted for 3.4% of global greenhouse gas emissions, a level comparable to the automotive industry
Around 8 million tons of plastic enter the oceans annually, endangering over 1,500 marine species
Over 400 million tons of plastic are produced annually, yet only 9% is recycled
